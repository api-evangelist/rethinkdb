We’re pleased to announce the availability of RethinkDB 2.4.4.  This
update gets the Data Explorer’s auto-complete working again, and it
carries some new compilation and platform-compatibility fixes.  For a
complete list of changes in this update, you can refer to the release
notes . Get involved As an open-source project that is developed and financially supported by its users, RethinkDB welcomes your participation. If there’s a feature or improvement that you would like to see, you can help us make it a reality. If you’d like to join us, there are many ways that you can get involved. Learn how to contribute to RethinkDB and find us on Slack , Discord , or Twitter .
