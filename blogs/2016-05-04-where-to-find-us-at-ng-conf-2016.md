We’re back at ng-conf in Salt Lake City for the second year in a row! If you’re
in town and want to know where to find us, here’s a quick breakdown of our
itinerary over the next few days. You can also find us at our booth just
outside the entrance to the Grand Ballroom. Wednesday May 4th 12:30pm: JavaScript Jabber Podcast live recording in the Grand Ballroom ( listen here! ) Thursday May 5th - Fair Day 10:00am - 10:30am: Ask Me Anything with co-founder Mike Glukhovsky at Hernitage 12:45pm - 1:00pm Newsroom interview livestream with Mike G. at Venizia 3:00pm - 4:00pm Horizon workshop Come build a chat app using Horizon and Angular in the Grand Ballroom A/D 5:00pm - 6:00pm Experts Room Let’s talk databases with Mike G. in the Audubon Friday May 6th Come check out our booth if you haven’t already! We look forward to meeting everyone and sharing Horizon with the Angular community.
If you’re having trouble finding us, tweet @rethinkdb and @horizonjs .
